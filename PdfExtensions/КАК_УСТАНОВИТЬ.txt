WHAT TO LOAD IN CHROME

Load the PdfExtensions project folder (the folder itself).

Path: Projects/PdfExtensions

Steps:
1. Open chrome://extensions/
2. Enable Developer mode (top-right).
3. Click "Load unpacked".
4. Select the PdfExtensions folder.
5. Confirm.

Usage: open any http(s) page, click extension icon, then choose "Download PNG" or "Download PDF".
