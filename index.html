Swiss Extensions

Page Capture, Tab Hibernate, Memory Cleaner, Site Blocker, and Site Data Clear — five tools in one side panel.

 01

Page Capture

Capture full pages by viewport tiles and export to PNG or PDF. Standalone extension.

 02

Tab Hibernate

Suspend inactive tabs and back up sessions to bookmarks. Standalone extension.

 03

Site Blocker

Block distracting websites with a minimal UI, custom lists, and NetFilter rules.

 04

Site Data Clear

Clear cookies, localStorage, and sessionStorage for the current site in one click.

 05

Tab Memory Cleaner

Discard background tabs to free RAM in one click, with optional pinned-tab protection.

 06

Video Downloader

Download videos from LinkedIn, Instagram, and Twitter. Paste a link to open the downloader.
